🍽 Regional Food — Takahara
Look for Mehari-zushi in your lunch box — rice balls wrapped in pickled mustard green leaves, a Kumano regional specialty.

The Takahara Kiri-no-Sato rest area near the guesthouse often serves fresh local citrus juice with a panoramic valley view — worth a stop on your walk.
