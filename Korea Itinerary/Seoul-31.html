Morning — COEX & Seongsu
Explore
Starfield Library & COEX Mall Gangnam / COEX  •  10:00 AM  •  Walk ~10–15 min from hotel

Starfield Library: A soaring three-storey open bookshelf covering an entire atrium wall, with reading chairs at floor level. Free entry; no ticket required. Browse freely and photograph in morning light.

COEX Mall: One of Asia’s largest underground malls — explore at a relaxed pace: aquarium atrium, K-pop merchandise stores, and lifestyle shops.

AdmissionFree
Depart by11:30 AM for Samsung Station (Line 2)
Transfer
COEX → Seongsu (Subway Line 2) Gangnam → Seongdong-gu  •  Depart 11:30 AM

Walk ~8 min from COEX Mall to Samsung Station (Line 2, follow underground mall corridor signs) → board Line 2 eastbound → ride 3 stops to Seongsu Station (~10 min) → Exit 3, walk ~8 min south down Yeonmujang-gil into the café district.

All Seongsu activities (Gentle Monster, Salt Bread, Cafe Onion, Rain Report Croissant, Seoul Forest) are walkable from this exit — no further transit needed until the 4:15 PM taxi to dinner.

Cost (2p, subway)~₩2,800
Arrive Seongsu~12:00 PM (door-to-door ~25–30 min)
Gallery
Gentle Monster — Haus Nowhere Seoul 433 Ttukseom-ro, 2F, Seongsu  •  12:00 PM  •  Allow ~45–60 min

Immersive art & retail experience blending futuristic sculpture installations with the brand’s eyewear collections. Each room is a full set-design environment — more art gallery than shop. Free entry; no ticket required.

Walk ~5 min south to Jayeondo Sogeumppang for the 1:00 PM salt bread stop.

AdmissionFree
Afternoon — Seongsu Cafes & Seoul Forest
Lunch & Cafes
Seongsu Lunch & Café Stops Seongsu-dong  •  1:00 PM  •  Allow ~90 min, all walkable

Jayeondo Sogeumppang (자연도 소금빵) — 56-1 Yeonmujang-gil. Buttery, flaky artisan salt bread; one of Seoul’s most talked-about bakeries. Grab a few to eat while walking.

Cafe Onion — Abandoned cement factory converted into a multi-level bakery: raw concrete walls, rusted staircases, and a rooftop terrace. Order a pastry and explore all floors.

Rain Report Croissant — Windows simulate rainfall every 15 min. Arrive between cycles to grab a table with a view. Check Instagram / Naver for current reservation method before departure (book March–April).

Est. Cost (2p)~₩30,000–₩50,000
PaymentCard accepted at trendy venues
Depart by2:30 PM — walk ~10 min north to Seoul Forest
Park
Seoul Forest (서울숲) Seongdong-gu  •  2:30 PM  •  Allow ~1 hr 45 min

Walk ~10 min north from the Seongsu café strip to the park’s south entrance. A large, peaceful green space ideal for a gentle stroll after a full morning.

Deer Garden: A small enclosure near the entrance where you can observe fallow deer up close — unexpectedly charming in the middle of a city.

Ttukseom Hangang Park: The forest connects to the Han River riverfront — walk to the water’s edge for a view of the river and the Seongsu Bridge.

AdmissionFree
Leave by4:15 PM — taxi to Geumdwaeji Sikdang (~20–25 min)

Dinner
