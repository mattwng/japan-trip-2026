Morning — Leeum Museum of Art
Transfer
Hotel Cappuccino → Leeum Museum of Art (리움미술관) Gangnam / Eonju → Hannam-dong, Yongsan-gu  •  Depart 10:00 AM

Taxi (recommended): ~15–20 min, ~₩10,000–₩15,000 via Kakao T. Tell the driver “리움미술관” (Leeum Misulgwan). No practical subway connection without two transfers and a long uphill walk — taxi is the clear choice.

Drop-off: Ask to be dropped at Samsung-ro 147-gil — follow the stone steps up from the street to the main gate.

Cost (taxi)~₩10,000–₩15,000
Museum
Leeum Museum of Art Hannam-dong  •  10:30 AM – 12:30 PM  •  Book tickets in advance
⚠ Book Tickets Online in Advance ticket.leeum.org — window opens May 19, 2026 at 12:00 AM KST (8:00 AM PDT). Select Combined Ticket (M1+M2+M3), senior rate ₩10,000/person. Permanent collection is free but M2 contemporary (Rothko, Paik) requires the paid combined ticket.
🌟 Pro Tip — Staircase Selfie Take a selfie in the White Rotunda (Botta building) spiral stairs — look up from the bottom for the rainbow aurora-film effect. The most-photographed spot in the museum.

Architecture — Three landmark buildings:

  • White Rotunda (Mario Botta): Sun-drenched spiral staircase descending through a funnel; aurora-film windows cast rainbow reflections.
  • Black Box (Rem Koolhaas): A massive black concrete volume that appears to float. Deliberately jarring against the white Botta building.
  • Rusted Steel (Jean Nouvel): Oxidized stainless steel shell makes the contemporary art inside glow by contrast.

Must-see Traditional (Museum 1): Goryeo Celadon (4F) • Joseon White Porcelain (3F) • Buddhist Art National Treasures (1F).

Must-see Contemporary (Museum 2): Mark Rothko “Untitled” • Nam June Paik multimedia installations • Anish Kapoor outdoor deck.

2026 Special — Tino Sehgal Live Art: “Constructed situations” throughout the galleries — performers may sing or dance without announcement. Intentionally startling; lean into it.

Admission (2p)~₩20,000 (senior rate, Combined M1+M2+M3)

Lunch
Itaewon Lunch Itaewon, Yongsan-gu  •  1:30 PM  •  Allow ~90 min

Pick one for a relaxed seated lunch:

  • Option A — Fusion: Vatos Urban Tacos — Kimchi Carnitas Fries are the signature dish; K-Mexican fusion done well.
  • Option B — Local: Jonny Dumpling — legendary pan-fried shrimp dumplings; order the crispy bottom set.
  • Option C — Trendy: Palette Hannam — viral omelet rice; visually striking and filling.

Finish by 3:00 PM — taxi back to hotel for afternoon rest.

Afternoon — Rest, then Yeonnam-dong & Hongdae
Rest
Afternoon Rest at Hotel Cappuccino 3:20 PM – 4:45 PM  •  ~85 minutes

Lie down, freshen up, and change before the evening. A full day at Leeum and Itaewon warrants a proper rest before heading out for dinner.

Depart hotel at 4:45 PM by taxi for Bungbung Honey Bee in Yeonnam-dong.

Transfer
Taxi: Hotel Cappuccino → Bungbung Honey Bee Gangnam / Eonju → Yeonnam-dong, Mapo-gu  •  4:45 PM

Taxi (Kakao T): ~25–30 min, ~₩20,000–₩25,000. Tell the driver “연남동 분봉 허니비” (Yeonnam-dong Bungbung Honey Bee). Arrive ~5:15–5:20 PM — a few minutes early to be seated for the 5:30 PM dinner.

Cost (taxi)~₩20,000–₩25,000
Dinner
Bungbung Honey Bee (분봉 허니비) — Yeonnam-dong Yeonnam-dong, Mapo-gu  •  5:30 PM  •  Walk-in

A honey-concept restaurant and bar in the heart of Yeonnam-dong — one of the neighbourhood’s most distinctive dining spots. The menu leans into honey pairings across food and drink. Relaxed, atmospheric. Allow ~75–90 min.

After dinner, walk ~8 min south into Hongdae for the busking scene.

Busking
Hongdae Walking Street Busking Hongdae Walking Travel Street (홍대 걷고싶은거리)  •  7:00 PM onwards

Walk ~8 min south from Bungbung Honey Bee down into the pedestrian busking corridor. By 7:00 PM the performance zones are in full swing: K-pop dance cover groups, indie singer-songwriters, and acoustic acts rotate through dedicated spots. Find a bench or a low wall to sit on.

The energy peaks around 8:00 PM. Depart when ready — a natural, low-effort capstone to the trip’s last full evening out.
